Welcome to Information about Exotic Fruit!

We are here to provide information about some of the exotic fruits of the world! The fruits displayed on our page are available in the United States but many people are unaware of the taste and benefits so we are here to provide insight about some these mystery fruits!

Please see the fruits below and read the specific descriptions for more in depth details about each fruit:

Cherimoya is a species of edible fruit-bearing plant in the genus Annona, from the family Annonaceae1. It is a heart-shaped, green fruit from South America, with a scaly, inedible peel and creamy, soft flesh inside. Cherimoya is also known as custard apple due to its creamy texture. It is native to frost-free higher elevations throughout tropical America and is widely cultivated in the Old World tropics for its pulpy edible fruits weighing about 0.5 kg (1 pound)

Durian is a tropical fruit with a spiky, hard outer shell and a pungent smell. It is native to Southeast Asia and is nicknamed the "king of fruits". The fruit can be large, weighing up to 7 pounds, and has a custard-like flesh with large seeds. The flesh can vary in color from yellow to white, red, green. Durian is used as food and medicine in Asia.

Lychee, also known as litchi or lichee, is a small tropical fruit12345. It has red scaly skin, sweet juicy pulp, and one large seed. The fruit is native to Southeast Asia and is popular in Asia, especially in China and Southeast Asia235. It is usually eaten fresh but can also be canned or dried.

Passion fruit is the fruit of the Passiflora vine, a type of passion flower. It has a tough outer rind and juicy, seed-filled center.There are several types that vary in size and color. Purple and yellow varieties are the most commonly available. Passiflora edulis - these are small round or oval-shaped fruits with purple skin. Passiflora flavicarpa - this kind is round or oval with yellow skin and usually slightly larger than the purple variety. Though they're a tropical fruit, some varieties can survive in sub-tropical climates.
Star fruit is a sweet and sour fruit that has the shape of a five-point star. The skin is edible and the flesh has a mild, sour flavor that makes it popular in a number of dishes. The star fruit is yellow or green in color. It comes in two main types: a smaller, sour variety and a larger, sweeter one.
